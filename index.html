At Kinibite, the culinary team takes pride in showcasing the diverse and vibrant flavors of Kenya.

  • The menu celebrates traditional Kenyan dishes with a contemporary twist, ensuring a memorable dining experience for guests.
  • From succulent nyama choma (grilled meat)
  • To aromatic pilau (spiced rice) and delicate ugali (maize meal), each dish is prepared using locally sourced ingredients of the highest quality.

The interior design blends contemporary elements with nods to Kenyan culture, creating a warm and stylish atmosphere.

Why Choose kinibite?

While honoring Kenyan cuisine, Kinibite also embraces global culinary influences. The menu features a fusion of international flavors, incorporating elements from around the world.

Learn More

International Fusion

From Asian-inspired stir-fries to Mediterranean-infused salads, the restaurant offers a diverse range of options that cater to different palates, providing something for everyone.

Exceptional Service

At Kinibite, attentive and friendly service is paramount. The staff is dedicated to creating a welcoming and enjoyable dining experience for every guest. Whether it's a special occasion or a casual gathering, the team at Kinibite goes above and beyond to make patrons feel valued and comfortable.

Farm-to-Table Ethos

Kinibite is committed to sustainability and supporting local farmers and producers. The restaurant prioritizes sourcing ingredients from nearby farms, ensuring freshness and minimizing its carbon footprint.
